The Cuida logo lockup — the brand symbol next to the lowercase "cuida" wordmark. Use it in nav bars, onboarding, footers and any brand moment.

```jsx
<CuidaWordmark size={26} variant="heart" color="var(--c-accent)" />
```

- `variant="heart"` is the primary mark (an ECG line inside a heart). `abraco` (two embracing circles) and `hand` are alternates.
- The wordmark is **always lowercase** — never "Cuida" or "CUIDA" in the lockup.
- On dark/accent backgrounds pass `color="var(--c-accent-fg)"`.
