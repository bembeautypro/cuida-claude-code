A small soft-tinted status label. Each tone pairs a semantic accent with its `-soft` background (e.g. alert ink on alert-soft).

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Em dia</Badge>
<Badge tone="alert" dot>Em falta</Badge>
<Badge tone="warn">Vence em 4 dias</Badge>
```

Keep labels short (1–3 words). Use `dot` for live/status meanings; omit it for plain category labels.
